I'm Ethan
aspiring aerospace engineer.

I'm a senior at Princeton High School in Cincinnati, Ohio with a deep passion for all kinds of engineering but especially aviation. What I love most isn't just the finished product, it's the process. The sketching, the building, the failing over and over until something finally works. To me, that's what engineering really is, turning an idea into something real, something functional, something that can actually help people.

Robotics

Robotics is my biggest focus outside of class. What I love about it is the environment, being around other people who think like me, bouncing ideas off each other, and getting to use my engineering mind freely. There's nothing like being in a room full of people who are just as excited about building things as you are.

Buisness

Outside of engineering, business is my other big passion. There's something about building a business and looking back and saying 'I did that' that drives me just as much as engineering does. In college I'm looking at minoring in finance or something similar to bring both sides together."
