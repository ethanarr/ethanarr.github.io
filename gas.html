Fireplace Safety System

The Challenge

Design and build a safety system for a fireplace that can detect dangerous conditions (like uncombusted gas) and automatically shut off the gas supply to prevent accidents or turn on a warning light based on the severity of the condition. The system should be reliable, cheap, and work in real-time to ensure safety.

The Research

This challenge was presented in my engineering class, given a real-world situation, we had to develop the logic using AOI gates. We worked through truth tables and K-maps to build out the logic, then designed and simulated the circuit in Multisim before physically building it on a breadboard with real components.

I'm taking this project further by building a full real-life version, substituting fire for water to safely recreate the simulation. I'll be presenting the completed system at the SkillsUSA State Competition for Principles of Engineering Technology in May 2026
