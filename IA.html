NACA 2421 Airfoil —
lift vs. temperature.

I studied whether air interacting with an airfoil at different temperatures would affect the amount of lift the airfoil produced. For this research project, I used a 3D-printed airfoil and a wind tunnel to test lift at different temperatures. I measured lift using pressure sensors and ran tests with the wind tunnel both indoors and outdoors to create a temperature difference.
